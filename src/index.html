About

Quadball (previously Quidditch)

is a full-contact, mixed-gender sport inspired by the game played in the Harry Potter books and movies. The game is played with two teams of seven players each, who play on a rectangular field with three goal hoops on each end. Players use brooms to run, while they use the four balls (a quaffle (volleyball) + three bludgers (dodgeballs)) and a snitch flag to score points. The game combines elements of rugby, dodgeball, and handball to create a fast-paced, high-energy sport that emphasizes teamwork, strategy, communication and physical fitness. Quadball has a growing international community and is played in regional, national and international competitions.

Team

The "Glacier Giants" were founded in 2019 and became Innsbruck's first Quadball team. In 2022 we played or first official games.
