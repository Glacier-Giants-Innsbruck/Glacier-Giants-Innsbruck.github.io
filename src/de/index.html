About

Quidditch

was originally created in 2005 at Middlebury College in Vermond.

The game of quidditch, commonly referred to as quadball, is played on a field the size of a hockey rink between two teams of at least seven players each who are all mounted on broomsticks.

Quidditch from J. K. Rowling's Harry Potter books served as inspiration for the sport, which was developed in 2005 at Middlebury College in Vermont.

Team

The "Glacier Giants" team was founded in 2018 and became Innsbruck's first Quidditch team. In 2022, the Ice Giants are officially registered to participate in international games.
